PRIVACY POLICY

Updated and Effective as of: July 4, 2018

We share your concerns about maintaining the integrity and privacy of personal information collected on the internet.  We are committed to protecting your privacy, and this privacy policy (“Policy”) is intended to describe our information collection and dissemination practices.

Please review the entire Policy and feel free to contact us using the contact information below if you have any questions.



COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

We will collect the following information from Facebook public pages:

- Statuses posted by the public page
- Aggregate counts of reactions to each status
- Anonymized user comments to each status
- Aggregate counts of reactions to each comment



USE OF INFORMATION

The information will be used to build sentiment analysis algorithms specific to Facebook data.



SHARING OF INFORMATION

This data will be used for research purposes only and will not be shared with any third parties.
